SMS Marketing:

Email open rates continue to decline, and the response rate to phone calls is below 6%. So it shouldn’t come as a surprise that the 98% open rates of text messages helps realtors to:

  • Keep in front of clients
  • Improve response rates
  • Generate more leads
  • Quickly find buyers


      • What’s even more important is that the vast majority of people want you to text them: a National Association of Realtors report found that 62% of all home buyers prefer their agent to send property info via text message. The good news is that only 5% of realtors currently communicate by text, so it’s an opportunity to delight customers and stand out from the crowd.
